Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20/12/2024

Dark Wallpaper we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our app. By using Dark Wallpaper, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. However, our app requires certain permissions for functionality:

  • Access Network State: To check network connectivity.
  • Internet Permission: To fetch wallpapers and display ads.
  • Post Notifications: To send app-related notifications.
  • Read Media Images: To access media files for wallpaper functionality.
  • Read/Write External Storage: To download and save wallpapers to your device.
  • Set Wallpaper: To directly set wallpapers on your device.
  • AD ID Permission: Required by Google AdMob for personalized ads.
  • Live Wallpaper Feature: Enables live wallpaper functionality.

2. How We Use Permissions

The permissions requested are solely used for the app’s functionality:

  • Internet permission is required for loading wallpapers and ads.
  • Storage permissions are used to save wallpapers directly to your device.
  • Set wallpaper permission allows the app to set wallpapers without leaving the app.

We do not access, store, or share any private data from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to improve the app experience:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data as per its privacy policy.
  • Firebase: For crash reporting (Crashlytics) and app configuration. Firebase may collect usage data to help us improve app performance.
  • OneSignal: For sending notifications about updates or new features.

Please review the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more details:

  • AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OneSignal Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not collect or share your personal data. However, third-party services like AdMob, Firebase, and OneSignal may process data as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

While we do not collect or store personal information, we prioritize security in our app’s infrastructure to ensure your data remains safe.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not target children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy within the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
